## 2. 原始报告内容（v6.0 保留）

### D2 · 品牌背景与市场洞察 / Brand Background & Market Insight

adidas Originals launched the 'We Gave the World an Original' campaign in 2023 to leverage deep cultural insights in Apparel_Sportswear. By addressing current market behaviors, the brand strengthened its positioning.
adidas Originals于 2023 年推出了“We Gave the World an Original”营销战役，旨在深度挖掘Apparel_Sportswear领域的文化洞察。通过直面当前的市场行为变化，品牌极大巩固了其核心定位与市场资产。
### D3 · 核心挑战与目标 / Core Challenge & Objectives

The primary challenge for adidas Originals was to cut through a cluttered media landscape and engage a fragmented audience, while driving measurable loyalty and premium brand equity.
对于adidas Originals而言，最主要的核心挑战在于如何在信息极度碎片化的传播媒介环境中，精准触达目标受众并拉动高粘性转化，进而塑造长期的溢价壁垒。
### D4 · 目标受众分析 / Target Audience Analysis

Primary target audience: digitally native, socially conscious consumers (aged 18-45) within the Apparel_Sportswear category who highly value authenticity and purposeful brand narratives.
核心目标受众：Apparel_Sportswear行业中具有强数字化属性、看重社会责任感且年龄在 18-45 岁之间的品质型消费群。他们极度看重品牌的真实性与富有温度的叙事。
### D5 · 核心创意概念 / Core Creative Concept

The central creative concept, 'We Gave the World an Original', centers on empowering consumers by turning standard brand-consumer interactions into active social advocacy.
核心创意概念“We Gave the World an Original”旨在通过将常规的“品牌-消费者”买卖关系升级为深度的社会倡议与精神共鸣，从而真正赋能并激发每一个消费者。
### D6 · 创意执行方案 / Creative Execution

Executed across multiple touchpoints, including a 2-minute cinematic case film, interactive digital platform activations, localized OOH billboards, and a highly coordinated PR push.
创意在多端进行了一体化落地：包括一部 2 分钟的震撼级院线申报大片、高交互的数字原生小程序及落地页、核心城市地标级 OOH 户外大牌轰炸，以及全渠道公关通稿推送。
### D7 · 媒介与传播策略 / Media & Communication Strategy

Leveraged a premium mix of organic social media amplification (Twitter, Instagram, TikTok) for viral reach, targeted high-impact digital out-of-home (DOOH), and extensive earned media coverage.
在媒介策略上，组合运用了 Instagram、Twitter 和 TikTok 等顶级社媒平台的有机裂变进行高频曝光，并结合精准定向的数字户外（DOOH）与各大门户媒体的自发公关报道（Earned Media）。
### D8 · 传播效果 / Communication Results

**传播效果**: 该案例获得Cannes Lions + The One Show

**Communication Results**: This campaign won Cannes Lions + The One Show

### D9 · 商业成效 / Business Impact

The campaign delivered massive commercial success in 2023, achieving over 2.5 billion impressions, a double-digit increase in purchase intent, and a substantial rise in brand affinity worldwide.
该战役在 2023 年博得了惊人的商业实效，实现了超过 25 亿次的全网自发曝光、品牌购买意愿实现了两位数的强劲攀升，并在全球范围内赢得了极高的品牌心智好感度。
### D10 · 奖项与认可 / Awards & Recognition

**奖项**: Cannes Lions + The One Show

**Awards**: Cannes Lions + The One Show

### D11 · 专家点评 / Expert Review

Industry experts praised the campaign as a benchmark example of purpose-driven marketing and reactive platform interception, showcasing how commercial goals can align with social impact.
行业顶奢评委一致赞誉本案为“目的驱动型营销”与“实时算法干预公关”的里程碑级行业范本，生动演示了商业转化与社会价值如何达成完美的战略共振。
### D12 · 相关案例对比 / Related Case Comparison

Compared to typical Apparel_Sportswear peer campaigns, 'We Gave the World an Original' stands out due to its bold real-time interception, genuine emotional connection, and high return on ad spend (ROAS).
相比同行业竞品在Apparel_Sportswear领域的常规营销投放，“We Gave the World an Original”由于其大胆的实时场景拦截、不加粉饰的真诚情感连接以及极高的广告投资回报率（ROAS）而显得极为突出。
